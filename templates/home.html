About the Journal Management System

JMS supports the full editorial lifecycle for academic journals published by the institution — from manuscript submission and peer review to editorial decisions and open publication. Authors can track their submissions, Reviewers can manage invitations, Editors can run the review workflow, and Administrators can oversee the whole system.
